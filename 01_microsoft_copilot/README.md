# Module 01 — Microsoft 365 Copilot for Oil & Gas

**Why this module matters:** Microsoft 365 Copilot is fundamentally different from ChatGPT. It lives **inside** your Outlook, Word, Excel and Teams — and has access to **your** emails, documents, calendar and meeting transcripts. The power comes from integration. The risk also comes from integration. A different threat model from public AI.

## ⚠️ Important: licensing & data residency

Microsoft Copilot for M365 costs **~USD 30 / user / month** (E3 or E5 add-on). Your IT team controls whether you have access. If your licence is not active, you can still:

1. Read the walkthroughs in this module
2. Watch the instructor's live demo
3. Run the **equivalent prompts** in ChatGPT or Claude using only **anonymised** data

**Data residency:** Most Pakistani enterprises route Microsoft tenants through **UAE or Singapore** regions. Confirm with your IT team **before** any production rollout — especially for SNGPL, SSGC, OGDCL and PPL, which fall under sensitive-sector classifications.

## 🎓 Learning outcomes

By the end of this module you will be able to:

1. Summarise a 20-email thread into a one-paragraph briefing and an action-item list
2. Convert raw HSE field notes into a structured 2-page incident report with a table of contents
3. Use natural-language formulas in Excel to flag production anomalies across hundreds of wells
4. Generate a Teams meeting recap with decisions attributed to specific attendees
