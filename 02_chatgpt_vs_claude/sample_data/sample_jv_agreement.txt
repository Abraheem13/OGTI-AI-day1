DRAFT JOINT VENTURE AGREEMENT
[OPERATOR-PK] and [FOREIGN-PARTNER]
For the Exploration and Development of [BLOCK-ID]
====================================================

PARTIES

This Agreement is made between:
(A) [OPERATOR-PK], a state-owned exploration and production company incorporated in Pakistan with registered office in [CITY], hereinafter "the Pakistani Party";
(B) [FOREIGN-PARTNER], a private exploration company incorporated in [JURISDICTION], hereinafter "the Foreign Party".

Together, "the Parties".

RECITALS

WHEREAS the Pakistani Party holds the exploration license for [BLOCK-ID] granted by the Government of Pakistan;
WHEREAS the Foreign Party possesses technical capability and capital required for development;
WHEREAS the Parties wish to form a Joint Venture for shared development of the block;
NOW THEREFORE the Parties agree as follows:

SECTION 1 — STRUCTURE OF THE JOINT VENTURE

1.1 The Parties shall form an unincorporated Joint Venture ("JV") effective from the Effective Date.
1.2 Participating Interests: Pakistani Party 40%, Foreign Party 60%.
1.3 The Foreign Party shall be the Operator for all exploration phases. The Pakistani Party retains the right to nominate the Operator from the development phase onwards, subject to demonstrating technical capability satisfactory to the Foreign Party.
1.4 The JV term is twenty-five (25) years from the Effective Date, renewable for two further ten-year terms at the option of the Foreign Party.

SECTION 2 — FINANCIAL COMMITMENTS

2.1 Initial Minimum Work Programme: USD 85 million, of which the Foreign Party shall contribute 100% in exchange for a deferred recovery from production proceeds at LIBOR + 4.5% per annum.
2.2 The Pakistani Party's share of subsequent costs shall be paid in cash within 30 days of cash call. Failure to meet a cash call within 60 days shall constitute a Default Event.
2.3 In the event of Default by the Pakistani Party, the Foreign Party shall have the right to increase its Participating Interest by an amount equal to the unpaid share of cumulative expenditure, valued at full historic cost plus a 50% uplift. Such adjustment shall be irreversible.
2.4 All accounts shall be maintained in USD. Any obligations payable in PKR shall be converted at the spot rate prevailing on the day of cash call, with no hedging obligation on the Foreign Party.

SECTION 3 — GOVERNANCE

3.1 An Operating Committee shall be established, comprising two representatives from each Party.
3.2 All decisions shall be made by simple majority of votes, with each Party's votes weighted according to its Participating Interest at the time of the vote.
3.3 The Foreign Party shall hold the casting vote in the event of a deadlock.
3.4 The following decisions require unanimous consent: change of Operator, sale of all JV assets, surrender of the licence.
3.5 All Operating Committee meetings shall be held in [FOREIGN-CITY], unless the Foreign Party agrees otherwise in writing.

SECTION 4 — DATA, INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND CONFIDENTIALITY

4.1 All technical data generated by the JV shall vest in the JV. The Foreign Party shall have the unrestricted right to use such data for the development of internal models, reservoir simulators, and proprietary databases, including for application to other blocks worldwide.
4.2 The Pakistani Party may use JV data only for operations relating to [BLOCK-ID] and may not share with other Pakistani operators or with the Government of Pakistan beyond what is statutorily required.
4.3 Confidentiality obligations under this Agreement survive termination for a period of fifteen (15) years.

SECTION 5 — TAXATION

5.1 All taxes payable in Pakistan in respect of the Foreign Party's income from this JV shall be borne by the Pakistani Party as a gross-up obligation, such that the Foreign Party's net post-tax return is preserved.
5.2 In the event of any change in Pakistani tax law that increases the Foreign Party's effective tax rate, the Pakistani Party shall make compensating payments within 30 days.
5.3 Withholding taxes on dividends or repatriation shall be borne by the Pakistani Party.

SECTION 6 — INDEMNITIES

6.1 The Pakistani Party shall indemnify the Foreign Party against all claims, damages, environmental liabilities and regulatory penalties arising from operations under this JV, save where directly attributable to the gross negligence or wilful misconduct of the Foreign Party as Operator.
6.2 The Pakistani Party's indemnity obligation under Section 6.1 shall not be subject to any cap or limitation in time or amount.
6.3 No cross-indemnity is provided by the Foreign Party.

SECTION 7 — TERMINATION

7.1 The Foreign Party may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing ninety (90) days' written notice, with no termination payment.
7.2 The Pakistani Party may terminate only for cause, defined narrowly as material uncured breach by the Foreign Party. Termination by the Pakistani Party requires payment to the Foreign Party of 200% of unrecovered investment.
7.3 On termination, the Operatorship and all field equipment shall be transferred to the surviving Party at book value less depreciation. Specialised proprietary software and reservoir models developed by the Foreign Party are excluded from such transfer.

SECTION 8 — DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 Any dispute arising under or in connection with this Agreement shall be referred to arbitration in [FOREIGN-CITY] under the rules of the [INTERNATIONAL-ARBITRATION-BODY].
8.2 The seat of arbitration shall be [FOREIGN-CITY]. The language shall be English. The governing law shall be the law of [FOREIGN-JURISDICTION].
8.3 Each Party waives any right to seek emergency relief or injunctive relief from any Pakistani court.

SECTION 9 — FORCE MAJEURE

9.1 Standard force majeure clause applies. Excused events include natural disaster, terrorism, declared war, and government action.
9.2 Government action by the Government of Pakistan that materially impairs the Foreign Party's economic position shall constitute a force-majeure event, entitling the Foreign Party to suspend obligations and seek compensation under Section 5.

SECTION 10 — MISCELLANEOUS

10.1 Most Favoured Nation: if the Pakistani Party enters into any subsequent agreement with any third party on terms more favourable than this Agreement, the Foreign Party shall be entitled to retroactive matching.
10.2 Assignment: the Foreign Party may freely assign its rights to any affiliate. The Pakistani Party may not assign without the Foreign Party's prior written consent.
10.3 Pre-emption: in the event of any sale of Participating Interest by the Pakistani Party, the Foreign Party shall have a right of first refusal.
