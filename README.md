# Practical AI for Oil & Gas — Hands-On Training Repository

**Day 1 | OGTI Islamabad | IBA CICT Executive Programme**

Welcome. This repository contains everything you need to complete the Day 1 hands-on labs — step-by-step instructions, sample data files, copy-paste prompts, interactive quizzes, and activities. Work through it at the pace your instructor sets.

---

## 🎯 What you will be able to do by the end of Day 1

1. Use **Microsoft Copilot** across Outlook, Word, Excel and Teams to triage emails, draft HSE reports, find production anomalies and recap meetings.
2. Compare **ChatGPT and Claude** side-by-side and choose the right one for SOPs, long-document review and incident investigation.
3. Write **professional prompts** tailored to your function — Operations, HSE, Procurement, or Engineering — using the 5-Part Anatomy (Role · Context · Task · Format · Limits).
4. Protect company and national data using the **Red List / Green List** and the Five Rules of Engagement (PECA 2016 aware).

## ⚠️ Before you start — the Golden Rule

> **If you wouldn't put it on a billboard in Blue Area, don't put it in a public AI tool.**

Pakistani state-owned energy data is a strategic asset. Unauthorised disclosure to foreign servers may attract liability under **PECA 2016 §3, §4 and §14**. Always read `resources/red_list_green_list.md` before pasting anything sensitive.
